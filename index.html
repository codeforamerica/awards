Why you should apply

Recognition

The Code for America Summit brings together leaders from hundreds of governments across the U.S. along with civic-minded technologists, designers, community organizers, and entrepreneurs. Awardees of the 2015 CfA Technology Award receive national recognition on the main stage and a unique opportunity to showcase the technology in front of prospective new users.

Endorsement

As a Government Technology Award recipient, awardees are endorsed by Code for America, and can list the awards in website and marketing materials.

Selection criteria

The Technology Awards will go to companies, government entities, civic technologists, and groups that are building software for the public sector in the United States. The software does not need to have been created in 2015. To be eligible for an award the technology must be:

  1. Currently used by at least one US-based government agency
  2. Available for other government agencies to use. For example, the technology could be available through well-documented open source code or available for purchase through a vendor.

A team member must be available to accept the award on-stage at the CfA Summit.

Additionally applicants will be selected based on their alignment with the Government Technology Principles, when applicable.
