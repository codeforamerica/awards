Recognizing excellence in government technology

The CfA Technology awards recognize outstanding examples of government technology products or implementations. Recipients are recognized on stage at the Code for America Summit.

Why you should apply

Recognition

The Code for America Summit brings together leaders from hundreds of governments across the U.S. along with civic-minded technologists, designers, community organizers, and entrepreneurs. Awardees of the 2015 CfA Technology Award receive national recognition on the mainstage and a unique opportunity to showcase the product in front of prospective clients.

Endorsement

As a Government Technology Award recipient, awardees are endorsed by Code for America, and can list the awards in website and marketing materials.

Selection criteria

The Technology Awards will go to companies, governments, civic technologists, and other groups who are building software for the public sector in the United States. The software does not need to have been created in 2015. To be eligible for an award your group must have:

  1. At least one US-based government agency must be using the technology

Additionally applicants will be selected based on their alignment with the Government Technology Principles, when applicable.
