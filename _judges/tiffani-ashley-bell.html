Tiffani Ashley Bell pursues better living through software. She is the co-founder and Executive Director of the Detroit Water Project, a platform connecting donors from around the world to people in Detroit and Baltimore needing assistance with water bills.

Since its founding in late July 2014, the platform has paid out over $225,000 in payments.

She was also a 2014 Code for America Fellow working with the City of Atlanta, GA to make doing business with the city easier. She was previously the CEO + founder of Pencil You In, enabling businesses as far away as Australia to operate more efficiently by accepting appointments online.

Tiffani is a front and back-end developer working primarily with Ruby on Rails and iOS. Tiffani holds a BS in Computer Science from Howard University.
